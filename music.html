Acoustic and Concert Music

Initial Impressions for two saxophones (2014)

Initial Impressions was written for saxophonists Jacob Swanson and Sarah Marchitelli in the summer of 2014. The movtive contours of this piece are in the shape of a J (for Jacob), and S (for Sarah). The title of this piece is a play on this motive structure based on the performer's intials.

Little Quartet for four guitars

Little Quartet was written in the Spring of 2013 for a masterclass with the Los Angeles Guitar Quartet. This piece has been performed over 30 times on the East coast of the United States, and has been useed at multiple universities for student quartets and chamber ensemble performances. This is one of Dan's most frequently performed pieces.
